How Your Website Reaches Customers (www.iymobiles.com)

For your customers to see your beautiful new website, it needs to be hosted online. Here's a quick guide to how that works:

1. Secure Your Web Address (Domain Name)

  • **What it is:** This is your unique website name, like `www.iymobiles.com`.
  • **How to get it:** You purchase a domain name from a "domain registrar" (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap). This is an annual registration.
  • **Why it's important:** It's how customers will find and remember your online store.

2. Choose Web Hosting

  • **What it is:** This is like renting space on a special computer (a server) that's always connected to the internet. Your website files (HTML, images, etc.) will live here.
  • **How to get it:** You sign up with a web hosting provider (e.g., Hostinger, Bluehost). For a simple HTML site like yours, "shared hosting" is usually affordable and sufficient.
  • **Why it's important:** It makes your website files accessible to anyone on the internet.

3. Upload Your Website Files

  • **What to upload:** All the files I've helped you create: your `index.html` file, and all the image files (which you'll update with your own product photos!).
  • **How to upload (Easiest Method):**
    1. Your web hosting provider will give you access to a **"Control Panel"** (often called cPanel).
    2. Inside, look for a **"File Manager"** tool.
    3. Navigate to the `public_html` or `www` folder (this is where your main website files go).
    4. Use the upload feature in the File Manager to transfer your `index.html` and all image files into this `public_html` folder.
  • **Alternative (for basic sites):** For static HTML sites, free services like GitHub Pages can host your site directly from a code repository.

Once these steps are completed, your customers will be able to visit `www.iymobiles.com` and explore IY MOBILES!
